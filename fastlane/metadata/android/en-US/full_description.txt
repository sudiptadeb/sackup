SackUp backs up your phone files (photos, videos, downloads) to a USB drive via OTG cable. No cloud services, no accounts, no internet required.

Features:
* Back up photos, videos, downloads, and WhatsApp media to USB drive
* Simple one-tap backup with large buttons designed for everyone
* Analyze what needs backing up before syncing
* Smart duplicate detection — skips files already on the drive
* Parallel file copying for faster transfers
* Foreground service with progress notifications
* Works completely offline — your files never leave your devices
* Free and open source
